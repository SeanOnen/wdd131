Site Name

PrecisionAg Hub

  • This name evokes a central "hub" for precision agriculture resources, emphasizing accessibility and innovation in biosystems engineering.

Optional domain availability: precisionaghub.com

Scenarios

  • As a small-scale organic farmer new to tech, I want to know which affordable IoT sensors are best for monitoring soil moisture in a vegetable garden, so I can optimize water use without over-investing.
  • As a biosystems engineering student researching for a thesis, I need access to case studies on drone-based crop monitoring in arid regions, including contact info for experts, to gather reliable data and references.

Typography

  • Open Sans (Bold for Headings): Used for h1-h3 headings to provide a professional, approachable tone with high readability.
  • Roboto (Regular for Body Text): Applied to paragraphs, lists, and general content for a clean, versatile body font that pairs well with sans-serif headings.

Site Purpose

The site serves as an educational and interactive hub for students, farmers, and engineers exploring precision agriculture within biosystems engineering. It provides in-depth information on tools and technologies, real-world case studies, downloadable resources, and a contact form for inquiries, aiming to promote sustainable farming practices through data-driven insights.

Color Schema

Primary Green (#4CAF50): Used for headings, buttons, and accents to symbolize growth and sustainability.

Accent Blue (#2196F3): Applied to links, navigation elements, and highlights for a tech-forward feel, representing precision and data visualization.

Supporting Light Gray (#f5f5f5): Background for the entire site to ensure readability and a clean, modern look.
